If you are a student or recent grad who want to be more deeply involved, you could also apply for our summer internships. Please submit your resume to info(at)fuudy.org with the title [Application_Name_Position]




Marketing Intern

You will help shape and maintain our brand image. Our impact depends directly on how effective our Marketing team is at driving brand awareness through effective marketing campaigns for each initiative. Examples of tasks could include launching product campaigns on social media, analyzing marketing data, or organizing and launching the marketing events. Previous knowledge of marketing is highly preferred but not necessary.


External Relationship Intern

You will help foster current and develop potential relationships with our sponsors. Our ability to fund our initiatives and continue operations is based mostly on sponsorship. In other words, you will manage our resources! Examples of tasks includes helping the team to identify and materialize the organization sponsorship with diplomacy and discretion. It is a great opportunity to network with industry professionals. Skill developed: Client relations, sales, and negotiation skills.


Creative Content Intern

Effective content is the back-bone of our success. You will use your creative mind to inspire the passion among our stakeholders. Examples of tasks include writing engaging contents on media and social event, analyzing competitor sites and bring up recommendations, develop marketing communication strategy.


Design Intern (work with our corporate partner)

You will work along with our design agency partner SPICE, who solves problems artistically, creatively, and innovatively. Examples of task include visual identities, graphics, marketing materials, website UI/UX. Skill required include Adobe Creative Cloud: AI, PS, and ID. We also seek your critical and creative thinking and problem-solving skills. UI/UX design and working experience is preferred but not necessary.
