Fuudy

Revive food, sharing, and lives beside you

Fuudy Movement

CANADA’S FIRST TECH-BASED CHARITABLE STUDENT INITIATIVE AGAINST HUNGER IN REVOLUTIONIZING THE WAY WE SHARE FOOD.

Fuudy utilizes technologies to revive food otherwise wasted and lives in hunger, by developing a sustainable perishable food redistribution system based on smart sharing fridge networks, connecting sharers and the needy, and simplifying food sharing within community.
